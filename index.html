SOME OF THE RIVERS IN INDIA

Indus river

The Indus river (Sindhu river),one of the longest river in Asia.The length of the river is 3,200 km, its Discharge: 6,600 m³/s. Mouths of this river is Arabian Sea, Nal sarovar Bird sanctuary. It travels accross china, pakistan, India.

The ganges river

The Ganga river is well known and declared as the National River of india. The length of the river is 2525 km. The source of the river is Nanda Kot, KEdarnath, Nanda Devi, Trisul, Satopanth Glacier, Gangotri Glacier, kamet.

Godavari river

The Godavari river, one of the sacred rivers of central India, near Nasik in Maharashtra.The source of the river is Brahmagiri Mountain. Bridges build over this river is Godavari Bridge, Godavari Arch Bridge.
